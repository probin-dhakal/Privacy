Barbify Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14/04/2025

1. Information We Collect

At Barbify, we respect your privacy. Here's what you need to know:

  • Device Identifier: We generate and store a unique device identifier (UUIDv4) to differentiate users and show your generation history (since we don't have login functionality). This anonymous identifier is only used for this purpose and for blocking users who violate our terms through unlawful activities.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or track personal data or images you upload.
  • Basic Device Information: We may collect anonymous device information (like OS version) to improve our app.
  • Consent Records: We store your consent to our terms for compliance purposes.

2. How We Use AI

Barbify uses Google's Gemini AI for image generation:

  • Your images are processed directly by Gemini and not stored on our servers.
  • Google may use submitted content to improve their models (see Google's Privacy Policy).
  • We implement strict content moderation to prevent misuse.

3. Your Responsibilities

  • Do not upload images containing personal or sensitive information.
  • You retain ownership of generated images but grant us limited rights to display them in your history.
  • Generated images are temporarily stored on our servers solely for the purpose of showing in your image history, and can be deleted by you at any time from the app's interface.

4. Children's Privacy

Barbify is designed for all ages but with special considerations:

  • Children under 5 should use the app with parental supervision.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without consent.
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's data.

5. Advertising Policy

We show ads to keep Barbify free:

  • All ads are family-friendly and manually reviewed.
  • We use contextual advertising without behavioral tracking.
  • No cookies or cross-site tracking technologies are used.

6. Data Security

  • All data transmissions are encrypted using SSL/TLS.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures.
  • Regular security audits are conducted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Significant changes will be notified in the app.
